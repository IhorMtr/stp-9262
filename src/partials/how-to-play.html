How to Play

  • Start Your Journey:
    Begin your trip from your home country and follow the travel map.

  • Solve Puzzles:
     Earn stars and coins by clearing objectives and using combos.

  • Collect Travel Tokens:
     Earn stars and coins by clearing objectives and using combos.

  • Unlock New Destinations:
     Complete location chapters to travel further and unlock new puzzle types.

  • Use Boosters:
     Deploy strategic boosters like Compass Clear or Suitcase Shuffle when things get tricky.

  • Celebrate Events:
     Participate in limited-time travel festivals and tours for special rewards.
