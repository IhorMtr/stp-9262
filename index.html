Match PUZZLE Travel

Match, Travel, Discover!

Description

  • Match Puzzle Travel is a captivating puzzle adventure game that takes players on a global journey filled with vibrant match-3 levels, famous destinations, and rewarding discoveries.

  • Match colorful tiles, complete travel-themed challenges, and unlock unique items from cities around the world.

  • Whether you're solving puzzles in Paris, collecting souvenirs in Cairo, or climbing puzzle paths in the Andes, this game blends the fun of matching with the thrill of travel.

  • Pack your bags — it's time to play your way around the world!

Advantages

  • Travel-Based Puzzle Gameplay:

    Each chapter is set in a real-world location with themed tiles and cultural flair.

  • Challenging Match-3 Mechanics:

    Enjoy classic line-matching fun with modern twists like shifting maps and region-based obstacles.

  • Unlock Souvenirs:

    Collect unique souvenirs and fun facts as you progress through each destination.

  • Cultural Puzzle Themes:

    Solve puzzles that incorporate cuisine, monuments, landscapes, and traditions.

  • No Wi-Fi Required:

    Play offline anywhere in the world — even on a real plane!

  • Limited-Time Travel Events:

    Participate in seasonal journeys (e.g. Sakura Season in Japan or Oktoberfest in Germany).

  • Daily Passport Bonuses:

    Log in daily to stamp your passport and earn in-game currency or boosters.

  • Adventure Mode:

    Follow a guided travel route unlocking new regions, puzzle variations, and hidden paths.

  • Relax or Compete:

    Choose between calm play or leaderboard travel tours.

  • Free-to-Play:

    Download and play completely free with optional upgrades.

Features

  • Match and Discover

    Match 3 or more tiles to complete goals and advance through real-world-inspired levels.

  • Interactive World Map

    Travel from one country to another on a dynamic animated map.

  • Boosters & Combos

    Use travel-themed boosters like Jet Lag Blasts, Compass Swaps, and Travel Time Skips.

  • Unlock Destinations

    Progress to new locations like Tokyo, Rome, Rio, and more.

  • Main Gameplay Features

  • Puzzle Postcards

    Earn collectible postcards after completing each major chapter.

  • Photo Album Mode

    View your global progress through a scrapbook-style album filled with souvenirs.

  • Event Pass

    Complete exclusive limited-time paths for rare rewards.

How to Play

  • Start Your Journey:
    Begin your trip from your home country and follow the travel map.

  • Solve Puzzles:
     Earn stars and coins by clearing objectives and using combos.

  • Collect Travel Tokens:
     Earn stars and coins by clearing objectives and using combos.

  • Unlock New Destinations:
     Complete location chapters to travel further and unlock new puzzle types.

  • Celebrate Events:
     Participate in limited-time travel festivals and tours for special rewards.

FAQ

  • Is Match Puzzle Travel free to play?

    Yes! The game is completely free with optional in-app purchases.

  • Can I play offline?

    Absolutely. All standard levels are playable without an internet connection.

  • Are the levels random or based on real places?

    Each chapter is inspired by a real-world city or culture, complete with visual references and fun facts.

  • How do I unlock new countries?

    Complete current location levels and collect enough stars to unlock the next region.

  • Is there a way to track my journey?

    Yes! Use your in-game travel album to view places you've completed and your collected souvenirs.

  • Will more destinations be added?

    Yes! New countries and cities are regularly introduced through updates.
