Advantages

  • Travel-Based Puzzle Gameplay:

    Each chapter is set in a real-world location with themed tiles and cultural flair.

  • Challenging Match-3 Mechanics:

    Enjoy classic line-matching fun with modern twists like shifting maps and region-based obstacles.

  • Unlock Souvenirs:

    Collect unique souvenirs and fun facts as you progress through each destination.

  • Cultural Puzzle Themes:

    Solve puzzles that incorporate cuisine, monuments, landscapes, and traditions.
